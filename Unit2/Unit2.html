The weapons in Terraria all differ in there ability. Most of the weapons are cassified into 4 catagory.

  • Melee
  • Ranger
  • Mage
  • Summoner

These four "classes" are used in the game to make the game more diffcult. But all of them have weapons that go with it. I want to talk about the Melee class of weapons.

The weapons that are most important in the game are has follows

  1. Wood swords
  2. copper - Platium sword (these are made from the beginning pre-hardmode ore)
  3. Blade of Grass, Fiery Greatsword, Lights bane/Blood Butcherer, Muramasa. these Four swords are used to make one sword called the Nights edge.
  4. Excalibur
  5. True Excalibur
  6. True Nights Edge
  7. Using the True Excalibur & True Nights Edge you can make the sword The Terra Blade
  8. The final sword thats considered the best sword in the game is called the Zenith

Thats the basic Melee weapons you can obtain in Terraria and some of the strongest ones aswell
