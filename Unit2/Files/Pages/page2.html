To mention the pre-hardmode basic ores swords there are 8 types of these swords. These swords are listed below.

  1. Copper Sword
  2. Tin Sword
  3. Iron Sword
  4. Lead Sword
  5. Silver Sword
  6. Tungsten Sword
  7. Gold Sword
  8. Platinum Sword

Each of these sword have a broadsword and shortsword versions
