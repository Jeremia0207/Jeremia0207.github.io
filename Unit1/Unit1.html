Basic Information on the Video Game Terraria

Terraria was a game released on May 16, 2011.

Creating the world:

Terraria world creation give sereval different options to make a world bigger or smaller.:

  • small
  • medium
  • large

Basic Tools and Different Ores:

These different world types give the player more resources and biomes the bigger the world is

Once you create the world your given the basic tools a copper sword, axe, and pickaxe.

The sword is use for killing the basic enemys that you come acrossed in the day time such as slimes, and the enemys at night like zombies.

The axe is used to chop wood.

The pickaxe is used to mine ores. All the ores can be mined by the copper pickaxe besides meteroite, demonite, hellstone, and crimtane ore. The pre-hardmode ores are as followed:

  1. Copper/Tin
  2. Lead/Iron
  3. Sliver/Tungsten
  4. Gold/Platium
  5. Demonite/Crimtane
  6. hellstone

The first 5 sets of ores are are divided into 2 ores each. Whenever a new world is created the first 5 ores are randomly added. For example when a world is created there a 50% chance you could have lead or iron in a world but you won't have both.

Hellstone is the last pre-hardmode ore you will get

Bosses:

Before you go into hardmode you need to fight mutiple different bosses. there is really not a order you need to go in besides the Wall of Flesh which is the boss you must beat to enter hardmode

The main pre-hardmode bosses are

  • Eye of Cthulhu
  • Eater of Worlds/Brain of the Cthulhu
  • Queen Bee
  • Skeletron
  • Wall of Flesh

Once you do defeat the wall of flesh that when the game gets more difficult.

NPC's

The final basic concept of Terraria would have to be the NPC

Terraria has a wide range of NPC you can get throughout your journy. NPCs themselves are just characters in the game that can sell you things or just give you advice. Some of the NPC you can obtain are listed below.

  1. Guide, which is the first NPC you can get and will give you advice along your journy. The guide is also sacrificed during the Wall of Flesh boss fight.
  2. Nurse, the nurse can heal you throughout your journy for a price though.
  3. Arms dealer, who sells bullets and ranged weapons for battle.
  4. Demolitionist, who sells bombs you can use to blow up the ground or ores.
